Welcome to the world of
Good Shepherd International School

GSIS is one of India’s best fully residential, international co-educational schools, located in Ootacamund in the Nilgiris. Our meticulous attention to academic excellence is matched by an inspiring range of extra-curricular activities that ensures the holistic development of every child under our care.

Our Camp Program

Scholastic Mastery

Embark on an intellectual odyssey with our Scholastic Mastery program, centered in International Relations and Business Management. Tailor your education with captivating electives like Personal Branding in the Age of Digital Footprints, Financial Literacy Workshop, and Creative Writing Intensive. Shape a future of knowledge, leadership, and creativity in our Scholastic Mastery program

Athletic Summit

Embark on an athletic journey like no other in the breathtaking Nilgiri Mountains! Our Sports/Athletic Summit is designed to offer a mix of thrilling and engaging activities that cater to all levels of skill and interest. From disc golf amidst nature’s beauty to the adrenaline rush of rifle shooting, this module promises an unforgettable summer experience.

Expeditionary Learning Retreat

Embark on a journey of self-discovery and skill development with our immersive 3-night outdoor expedition. This module is meticulously designed to equip campers with essential survival skills, foster a spirit of adventure, and instill a deep appreciation for the natural world.

Music/Dance and Harmony Workshop

Elevate your summer with the sound of music and the joy of dance! Our Music/Dance and Harmony Workshop offers a diverse range of musical explorations and dance experiences, suitable for all levels of talent and interest. Whether you’re a budding musician or a dance enthusiast, this module is tuned to your frequency.
